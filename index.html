Hidden-button


Rules of the game:

To find the winning button you have to click on the displayed buttons (at first you will find only 3 buttons).
When you press a random button, a message will appear on the screen telling you if that button is a winner or a loser.
If he is the winner, then it will change to green color or if he is the loser, then it will change to red color.
In addition, you will find an input where you can generate several buttons and look for the winning one.
